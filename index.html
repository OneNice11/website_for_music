WE LOVE MUSIC

It all begins at a water theme park; Ibiza's Aqualand on Playa d'en Bossa.

Three mates are sipping acid bright fizzy drinks, and yearning for their first kiss when three young English girls accost them. They suggest going to listen to some music, and the boys follow them hastily, convinced they'll score sometime between a baile flamenco and a paso-doble. They are soon hurled into a room rammed with thousands of people and realise that David Morales neither is a guitar player, nor a flamenco singer.

This new and unexpected experience was a revelation for them.

Back from their holidays, they pose naked for Car Tuning magazine and blackmail their parents for the funds to buy their first BST decks. Fast forward past university and first jobs, they're proud to spend all their hard-earned cash on antique synthesizers bought on Ebay or from Audiofanzine. They continue their passion for House over beers and peanuts, talking about the label that - they promise themselves - they'll found one day.

And so here we are. A few years later, the label is born.

Cheers from France
